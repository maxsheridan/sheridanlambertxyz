Sheridan Lambert is the alias of graphic designer Tug Wells and pulp fiction writer Max Sheridan. He is the inventor of Pay Python, a utility that allows Venmo users to absorb hostile accounts, and Buzzer Buddy, a prosthetic enhancement that gave players a minor competitive advantage in Family Feud’s discontinued thunder rounds. He is an honorary member of the RGR Nabisco Group and the director of the Maurice Gibb Estate. He lives in Bern, Switzerland.
